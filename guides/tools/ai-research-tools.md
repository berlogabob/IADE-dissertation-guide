# AI Research Tools

## Overview

AI tools can accelerate your literature review by helping you find, read, and synthesize research papers faster. Use them as aids, not replacements for critical reading.

## Research Discovery

### [Consensus](https://consensus.app/)
- Ask research questions in natural language
- Returns answers backed by peer-reviewed papers
- Shows consensus level across studies

### [Elicit](https://elicit.com/)
- Analyzes research papers automatically
- Extracts methods, findings, and limitations
- Creates comparison tables from multiple papers

### [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/)
- AI-powered search with TLDR summaries
- Citation context (how papers cite each other)
- Research feeds based on your interests

### [Perplexity Academic](https://www.perplexity.ai/academic)
- AI search with academic sources
- Provides citations for every claim
- Good for initial exploration

## Citation Analysis

### [Scite](https://scite.ai/)
- Shows how papers are cited (supporting, contrasting, mentioning)
- Helps evaluate paper impact and reliability
- Citation context analysis

### [Inciteful](https://incitefulmed.com/academic/)
- Free citation graph analysis
- Find papers that cite a given paper
- Identify citation networks

## Visual Discovery

### [Connected Papers](https://www.connectedpapers.com/)
- Visual graph of related papers
- See influence networks
- Free: 5 graphs/month, Premium: unlimited

### [Research Rabbit](https://app.researchrabbit.ai/)
- Free unlimited citation graphs
- Track collections of papers
- Email alerts for new related papers

### [LitMaps](https://app.litmaps.com/)
- Dynamic citation maps
- Real-time updates when new papers are published
- Free and premium tiers

## Important Notes

- **Always verify** AI-generated claims with original sources
- **Critical reading** is still essential
- Check for **hallucinations** (AI making up papers or findings)
- Use AI as a **starting point**, not the final word
- Follow your institution's **AI usage policies**

## When to Use AI Tools

| Stage | Tool | Purpose |
|-------|------|---------|
| Discovery | Consensus, Elicit | Find relevant papers |
| Screening | Semantic Scholar, Scite | Evaluate paper quality |
| Mapping | Connected Papers, Research Rabbit | Visualize the field |
| Synthesis | Elicit, Consensus | Compare findings |
