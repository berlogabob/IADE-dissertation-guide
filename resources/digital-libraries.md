# Digital Libraries & Repositories

Academic databases for finding peer-reviewed papers, conference proceedings, and technical reports.

## General Search

| Name | URL | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/) | Scholar | Broad coverage, free |
| [Google Scholar AI](https://scholar.google.com/scholar_labs/search) | Scholar Labs | AI-enhanced search |

## Computer Science & Engineering

| Name | URL | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| [ACM Digital Library](https://dl.acm.org) | ACM | Top CS publications |
| [IEEE Xplore](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/Xplore/home.jsp) | IEEE | Engineering, CS |
| [dblp](https://dblp.uni-trier.de/) | dblp | CS bibliography, proceedings |
| [ScienceDirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/browse/journals-and-books?subject=computer-science) | Elsevier | CS, Engineering |

## Multi-Disciplinary

| Name | URL | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/) | AI2 | AI-powered, free |
| [Springer Nature](https://link.springer.com/) | Springer | Journals, books, protocols |

## Preprints & Open Access

| Name | URL | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/archive/cs) | arXiv | CS preprints, not peer-reviewed |
| [Zenodo](https://zenodo.org/) | CERN | Papers, software, datasets |
| [Sci-Hub](https://www.sci-hub.in/) | — | Paywall bypass, use cautiously |

## Tips

- **Search multiple databases** — No single source covers everything
- **Use advanced search** — Combine keywords with AND, OR, NOT
- **Set up alerts** — Get notified when new papers match your query
- **Check citations** — Follow the citation chain forward and backward
- **Use Interlibrary Loan** — Request papers your library doesn't have
