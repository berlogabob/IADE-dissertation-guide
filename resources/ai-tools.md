# AI Research Tools

Artificial intelligence tools to help with research discovery, analysis, and synthesis.

| Tool | URL | Cost | Purpose |
|------|-----|------|---------|

## Guidelines for Using AI Tools

### Do
- Use as a **starting point** for discovery
- **Verify** all claims with original sources
- **Cite** papers you find through AI tools
- Follow your institution's **AI policies**

### Don't
- Trust AI-generated summaries without reading the paper
- Use AI to write your literature review
- Assume AI-found papers are all relevant
- Skip critical evaluation of sources
